EVB-LAN9662-EndNode (EV23X71A)

The EVB-LAN9662-EndNode (Microchip P/N: EV23X71A) is a 2-port switch capable of running VelocityDRIVE-SP.

Here is what the board looks like:

  1. 1 Gb/s (1000BASE-T) Ethernet port 1 and 2

  2. USB UART Console

  3. Reset button

  4. Boot-mode DIP-switch (strapping modes)

All connectors and controls not listed here are not supported by the VelocityDRIVE-SP Firmware.
